A hundred questions across the whole course, Parts 1 through 5, in roughly the order you learnt them. Each one is checked the moment you answer: you see at once whether you were right — and why — but you cannot change an answer or step back, only move forward. At the end you get your score and a link you can keep to share your result or review what you missed.
