title: Adverbs
---

{% include menu.html %}

An _adverb_ is an option that changes how a regex behaves. You write it as a colon-word at the start of the pattern (or on the `m///` operator). Adverbs let you match without regard to letter case, find **all** the matches instead of just the first, and make spaces in the pattern significant.

This section covers the three you will reach for most often: `:i`, `:g`, and `:s`.
