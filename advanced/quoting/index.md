{% include menu.html %}

In the Essentials part you used [single and double quotes to write strings](/essentials/strings). Those are the two most common members of a whole family of _quoting constructs_ that Raku gives you for building strings with exactly the amount of processing you want — from “take every character literally” to “interpolate variables, escapes, and even embedded code”.

This section covers the three base forms `q`, `qq`, and `Q`, the adverbs that fine-tune them, and _heredocs_ for multi-line text.
