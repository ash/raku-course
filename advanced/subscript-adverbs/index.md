title: Subscript adverbs
---

{% include menu.html %}

When you subscript an array or a hash — `@a[2]`, `%h<key>` — you can attach an _adverb_ to change what the subscript does. Instead of just fetching the value, you can ask whether a key exists, delete it, or get the key and the value together.

These adverbs are written after the subscript with a colon, and they work the same way on both arrays and hashes. This section covers the most useful ones.
