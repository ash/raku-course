## Problem

A single `[*]` reduction gives one factorial. Your task is to print _every_ factorial from _1!_ up to _6!_ on a single line, in order, using one reduction. Use the triangular form of the reduction meta-operator so that all the intermediate products are kept, not just the final one.

## Example

The program prints the list of factorials of 1 through 6:

```
(1 2 6 24 120 720)
```
