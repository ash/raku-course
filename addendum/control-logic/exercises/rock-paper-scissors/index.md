## Problem

Judge rounds of rock–paper–scissors. For the match-ups
`rock`/`scissors`, `paper`/`rock`, and `paper`/`paper`, print which player wins,
or `tie` when the moves are equal.

## Example

The program prints:

```
rock vs scissors: rock wins
paper vs rock: paper wins
paper vs paper: tie
```
