## Problem

For each of the numbers `-4`, `0`, and `7`, print whether it is negative, zero,
or positive, and whether it is even or odd, in the form shown in the example.

## Example

The program prints:

```
-4 is negative and even
0 is zero and even
7 is positive and odd
```
