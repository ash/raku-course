title: Live and on-demand supplies
---

{% include menu.html %}

Supplies come in two flavours, and knowing the difference saves a lot of confusion. An _on-demand_ supply starts producing its values afresh for **each** tap, like replaying a recording. A _live_ supply broadcasts values as they happen to **all** current taps, like a radio station — if you tune in late, you miss what already aired.

This section shows both kinds and when each one is what you want.
